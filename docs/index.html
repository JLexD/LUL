Features

Easily access Twitch from Discord

View details and stats from your favorite streamers on Twitch. Get current view and stream status, current game, and a preview of the stream.

See what's trending

See the most popular games and streams on Twitch with just one command. You'll also see the amount of channels streaming and total current views for the game.

Listen in voice chat

You can listen to any live Twitch stream in voice chat with all of your friends. This feature is only available to people who upvote TwitchBot on DBL.

Get notified when channels stream

Get an in-chat notification when your favorite streamers go live with a single command. Just specify the channel you want the notification in, and the streamer you want.

View player stats from your favorite games

new! Take a look at any player's Fortnite, Overwatch, or Rocket League stats by entering their in-game name. See a wide variety of stats for each game.

Get highlights and clips

new! Search Twitch's wide selection of clips recorded by tons of users. Search by trending, a certain game, or a Twitch streamer.
